# Federal arts funding

Datasets covering US federal arts and humanities funding: NEA, NEH, IMLS, Smithsonian, plus federal pandemic-recovery arts allocations (ARPA, CARES, RESTART).

## Primary sources

- **National Endowment for the Arts — Research & Analysis** — https://www.arts.gov/impact/research
- **NEA grants data** — https://www.arts.gov/grants/recent-grants
- **NEA American Rescue Plan documentation** — https://www.arts.gov/COVID-19/the-american-rescue-plan
- **National Endowment for the Humanities — Funded Projects** — https://www.neh.gov/grants/recent
- **Institute of Museum and Library Services — Funded Projects** — https://www.imls.gov/grants/awarded-grants
- **U.S. Treasury — State and Local Fiscal Recovery Funds** — https://home.treasury.gov/policy-issues/coronavirus/assistance-for-state-local-and-tribal-governments/state-and-local-fiscal-recovery-funds
