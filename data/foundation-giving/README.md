# Foundation giving

Datasets covering private, community, corporate, and operating foundation giving to the arts and culture sector.

## Primary sources

- **Candid (Foundation Center)** — https://candid.org/
- **IRS Form 990-PF filings** for major arts and culture foundations — https://www.irs.gov/charities-non-profits/form-990-resources-and-tools
- **Giving USA** — annual report on charitable giving
- **Urban Institute — National Center for Charitable Statistics** — https://nccs.urban.org/
- **Americans for the Arts** — sector reports — https://www.americansforthearts.org/
