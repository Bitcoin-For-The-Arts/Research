# Articles

Long-form research articles by **Bitcoin for the Arts Research.** These are mirrors of or precursors to formal reports — published on BFTA's Substack and other channels as editorial framings, then revisited in this folder as canonical, citation-tightened source material.

The relationship between articles, reports, and newsletters:

| Channel | Purpose | Standard |
|---|---|---|
| **Article** (here, and on Substack) | Long-form editorial framing of a research thesis | Sourced, narrative, accessible voice |
| **Report** ([`../reports/`](../reports/)) | Canonical, citable, footnoted research document | Federal-statistical-agency rigor |
| **Newsletter** ([`../newsletters/`](../newsletters/)) | Short, scheduled update on BFTA work | Reports a fact or a development; links to sources |

## Layout

Each article lives in its own folder:

```
articles/<slug>/
├── README.md            ← cover sheet: title, byline, publication date, where published, abstract
├── article.md           ← full text
├── sources.md           ← primary-source list with permalinks
└── figures/             ← optional charts/images used in the article
```

## Citation standard

Articles follow the same citation discipline as reports — see [`../CITATIONS.md`](../CITATIONS.md). The voice may be more narrative than a report, but every factual claim must still trace to a primary source.
