# The Blueprint David Simon Left Behind — editorial article

**Byline:** Bitcoin for the Arts Research
**Channel:** Substack
**Status:** Editorial framing
**Read time:** ~6–7 minutes (~1,200 words)
**Working title (alternate):** *My High School Built a 501(c)(3) Inside a Public School in 1979. I'm Building One on Bitcoin in 2026.*

## Abstract

A case-study editorial centered on the Baltimore School for the Arts (BSA) — a 501(c)(3) foundation embedded inside a public school that was designed in 1979 as a workaround for the chronic underfunding of public arts education. Argues that BSA is the structural template Bitcoin For The Arts is rebuilding on Bitcoin rails in 2026, and uses BSA's funding history to set up the broader case for sound-money-based, non-state-dependent arts patronage. Companion piece to *The ARPA Cliff*.
