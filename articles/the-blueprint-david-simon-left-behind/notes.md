## What NOT to do

- ❌ **Don't publish all 3 Notes at once.** The Mon → Wed → Thu rhythm is what makes the algorithm push compound. All-at-once dumps lose 70% of the lift.
- ❌ **Don't add an Art + Zap Weekend CTA to any of these Notes.** This article is a trust-building piece, not a conversion piece. Save Art + Zap pitches for a different week. Mixing them cheapens both.
- ❌ **Don't tag people who weren't actually involved in the article.** Tagging random arts orgs hoping for a share reads as spam. Tag only entities/people genuinely connected to the content (BSA, Mark Joseph, the alumni network).
- ❌ **Don't argue with critics in the comments.** If someone responds critically, thank them for engaging and let it sit. The article speaks for itself; defensive arguing erodes the trust the article built.
